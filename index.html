--- layout: default title: About Sahit ---

Hi, I'm Sahit! I build intelligent robots.

I'm a third year studying Computer Science at Georgia Tech with concentrations in Artificial Intelligence and Theoretical Computer Science. My interests include motion planning for robotics, reinforcement learning, and online learning algorithms. I am secretary of The Agency, Georgia Tech's Machine Learning research club for undergraduates. I am a software instructor and former software lead for RoboJackets. My research is currently in the Robot Learning Lab where I look at the application of Gaussian Proccesses in real-time motion planning. I am also associated with the Search Based Planning Lab at Carnegie Mellon University where I was a part of the 2018 cohort of Robotics Institute Summer Scholars. When I'm not playing with robots, I like to read, listen to music, play tennis, shoot photos, and go for runs.
