Hi, I'm Sahit! I build intelligent robots.

I'm a recent graduate of the College of Computing at Georgia Tech. While I was a student I took on the role of Internal Operations manager for The Agency, Georgia Tech's Machine Learning research club for undergraduates. I also was a former software lead for RoboJackets, Georgia Tech's competitive robotics team.

My interests include motion planning for robotics, reinforcement learning, and online learning algorithms. I studied these topics in the Robot Learning Lab where I worked on projects including using Gaussian Proccesses for online planning and Model-Predictive Control algorithms for autonomous vehicles.

Previously, I have worked with the Search Based Planning Lab at Carnegie Mellon University as a part of the Robotics Institute Summer Scholars program. I also interned in the Human-Centered Robots Lab at the University of Washington where I was an intern for the summer of 2019.

When I'm not playing with robots, I like to read, listen to music, shoot photos, boulder, and play tennis/squash/ping pong.
